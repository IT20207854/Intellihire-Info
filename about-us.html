About Us


Intellihire was developed by a team of final-year undergraduate students at SLIIT as a result of their final-year project. This system was developed with the aim of making the recruitment process in the IT industry more efficient, cost-effective and less time-consuming by easily shortlisting candidates applying for a specific position.


Intellihire's development involved the implementation of state-of-the-art machine learning and NLP algorithms to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of the recruitment process, enabling the system to make data-driven decisions and extract valuable insights from textual data. These advanced technologies help Intellihire stand out as a powerful tool for streamlining and improving the hiring process in the IT industry.
